# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, fellow developers, and open-source collaborators looking at Gyanendra Prakash's engineering history, projects, and skills.

## Product Purpose
A high-fidelity minimalist portfolio that highlights Gyanendra's expertise in software engineering, AI/LLMs, and hardware projects.

## Brand Personality
Editorial minimalism, monospace-forward typography, structured layout grids, tactile feedback, and subtle high-performance transitions.

## Anti-references
- Cliché SaaS landing pages (e.g. giant glowing neon cards, generic metrics grids)
- Over-rounded card styling (border-radius > 16px)
- Glassmorphism templates with heavy backdrop blur filters everywhere
- Section eyebrows on every single heading (e.g., "01 / About", "02 / Skills" on every single page block)

## Design Principles
1. **Content First**: Hierarchy is established by scale and weights, not by unnecessary card containers.
2. **Tactile Response**: Interactive elements must respond to pressure and hover with high-fidelity transitions.
3. **Respect user options**: Support seamless theme changes and prefers-reduced-motion preferences.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio ≥ 4.5:1 for body and labels.
- Native keyboard access and reduced-motion support.
